Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA)

An introduction to the purpose and importance of the course.

About the Course

This course focuses on designing efficient algorithms, analyzing their computational complexity, and applying them to solve real-world problems. It covers various algorithmic paradigms like divide-and-conquer, greedy techniques, dynamic programming, and backtracking.

Purpose of the Course

The primary purpose is to equip students with the skills to think logically, develop optimized solutions for problems, and connect theoretical concepts with practical applications.

Applications

DAA has numerous real-world applications, including optimization in networks, pathfinding in navigation systems, and efficient resource allocation in various industries.
